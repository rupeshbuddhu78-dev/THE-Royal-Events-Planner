Turning Dreams Into Royal Realities

Welcome to The Royal Events Planner. Located in the heart of Cooch Behar at Debi Bari, Nuta Para, we bring over a decade of rich experience in making celebrations truly unforgettable. We believe that every event, big or small, deserves a touch of royalty, elegance, and absolute perfection.

From grand weddings and elegant corporate gatherings to intimate birthday parties and rice ceremonies, our expert team handles every detail with precision. With 10+ years of trusted service, we pride ourselves on our premium catering, stunning floral decorations, and stress-free management, allowing you to enjoy your special day as a guest at your own party.
